Where to Begin? A Quick Look at Knitting Tools

Welcome to the Knitting Community! So the question is, what do you need to get started? Here are a few of my suggestions:

Start with these:

Straight Knitting Needles:

The Straight Needle is your basic knitting needle. They are usually made of metal, but you can also find them in bamboo or plastic. I love the bamboo knitting needles. Start by getting a size that will work with a variety of yarn weights. I would recommend sizes 7-9.

Yarn of your choice:

There are so many different types of yarn, from your cheapo red heart scratchy acrylic to your uber-expensive angora wool lace yarn. When starting out, choose a yarn whose texture you like that is within your price range. I’m personally a big fan of alpaca blends.

A ruler:

A ruler is super important when evaluating your gauge. This will be important when working in a pattern.

Are you liking it so far? Now get these:
  1. Expand your collection of straight needles
  2. Circular Needles
  3. Stitch Markers
  4. Gauge Markers
Now you’re getting there! These are next on your list:
  1. Double Pointed Needles
  2. Cable Needles
